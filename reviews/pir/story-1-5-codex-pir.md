**Overall**  
`REVISIT`

The story is directionally correct and it does establish the essential downstream data contract, but the actual stage gate is not yet aligned with the system’s real V1 success criteria: reproducibility, evidence quality, and operator confidence. The main reason is not missing detection logic; it is that some of the most important findings are not carried through into the artifact and gate behavior.
