**2. Simplification**  
Assessment: `ADEQUATE`

- The core mechanism is not over-engineered. Writing canonical Arrow for compute plus Parquet for cold storage is the architecture, not invention for invention’s sake; the same is true of crash-safe writes and mmap verification in [`architecture.md`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/architecture.md#L340) and [`1-6-parquet-storage-arrow-ipc-conversion.md`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/_bmad-output/implementation-artifacts/1-6-parquet-storage-arrow-ipc-conversion.md#L557).
- There is avoidable complexity in path/config resolution. Storage can come from `[data].storage_path`, `[data_pipeline].storage_path`, or `[data_pipeline.storage]` in [`base.toml`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/config/base.toml#L5), [`base.toml`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/config/base.toml#L65), and [`base.toml`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/config/base.toml#L73), and the converter layers fallbacks over all three in [`arrow_converter.py`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/src/python/data_pipeline/arrow_converter.py#L65). A single explicit storage root or a single explicit Arrow/Parquet pair would be simpler for a non-coder operator.
- Contract discovery is also more implicit than it needs to be. `_find_contracts_path()` walks `cwd()` and parents in [`arrow_converter.py`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/src/python/data_pipeline/arrow_converter.py#L78). That is operationally fragile; an explicit project-root or config-resolved contracts path would be simpler and more reproducible.
- One abstraction appears redundant in the runtime path: `validate_dataframe_against_schema()` is imported in [`arrow_converter.py`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/src/python/data_pipeline/arrow_converter.py#L27), but conversion actually relies on `pa.Table.from_pandas(..., schema=...)` plus a post-build schema equality check in [`arrow_converter.py`](/C:/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex%20Pipeline/src/python/data_pipeline/arrow_converter.py#L194). That extra validator increases surface area without changing the stage’s actual behavior.

**Overall**  
`REVISIT`

The core storage work is architecture-aligned and useful, but the persisted stage contract is not clean enough yet: the CLI handoff from validation is mismatched, the new config knobs are not schema-validated, and the manifest does not fully carry the lineage/reproducibility proof that later stories expect.
