**2. Simplification**  
Assessment: `ADEQUATE`

- The parse/default/generate/orchestrate split is reasonable for testability; this is not abstraction for abstraction’s sake. The bigger simplification opportunity is eliminating duplicated config-resolution paths. One config-driven source would achieve the same goal with less drift risk.
