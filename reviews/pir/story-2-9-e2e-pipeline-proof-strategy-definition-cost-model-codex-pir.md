Concrete observations:
- A simpler design would be: generator emits the full spec contract natively, confirmation marks the same artifact as final, and both Rust crates consume that exact confirmed artifact. That would remove enrichment, static side paths, and known-gap filtering from the proof.
- The current xfailed structured-log check is scaffolding, not value. Either the production path should populate `LogContext` and the proof should assert it, or the requirement should stay out of this story until real support exists.
- Saving the summary artifact during the proof would give more operator value than the current in-memory summary plus a lightweight proof manifest.
