**2. Simplification**

Assessment: `CONCERN`

- The `selection/` subsystem itself is not the problem; the architecture explicitly moved this out of a single candidate-compressor file because Growth-phase selection is bigger than that. ([architecture.md:830](/c/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex Pipeline/_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/architecture.md#L830), [architecture.md:1717](/c/Users/ROG/Projects/Forex Pipeline/_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/architecture.md#L1717))
