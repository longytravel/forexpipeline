## Overall Verdict
VERDICT: RETHINK

The capability is aligned with Growth-phase goals, but the current story mixes two different pipeline positions, breaks the documented architecture, and under-specifies the operator/reproducibility contract.

## Recommended Changes
1. Decide whether this story is pre-validation candidate compression or post-scoring forward-test basket selection, and rewrite the story to one position only.
2. If it stays pre-validation, remove PBO, cost-stress, and Story 5.5 scoring-manifest dependencies from AC3, Task 4, Task 7, and the upstream contract.
3. Remove `SELECTING` / `SELECTION_COMPLETE` state-machine changes unless you also revise D3 and add the missing evidence-pack and `/pipeline` review workflow.
4. Align the implementation location with D11 by making this an analysis-layer artifact, or explicitly amend architecture before implementation.
5. Expand `SelectionManifest` to include upstream artifact refs, optimizer config/version, dataset/spec/cost-model hashes, decision-matrix values, CRITIC weights, gate outcomes, excluded-candidate reasons, and RNG seed.
6. Add acceptance criteria for deterministic reruns producing identical cluster IDs, rankings, selected sets, and manifest hashes.
7. Resolve the memory model; replace the contradictory 2.4GB-vs-50MB claim with an explicit chunked or approximate strategy and tests around NFR4 compliance.
8. Add operator-facing evidence outputs: selection evidence pack, review entry point in `/pipeline`, and a decision trace comparable to Story 5.5.
9. Consider a simpler first Growth implementation: deterministic clustering plus one or two representatives per cluster, deferring MAP-Elites and exploratory sampling.
10. Keep HDBSCAN if the research stands, but make the D11 update a prerequisite architectural change, not an incidental side effect of story completion.
